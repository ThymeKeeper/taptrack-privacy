TapTrack — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

TapTrack (“the app”) is a GPS waypoint logger. This policy explains what the app accesses and how your data is handled.

What the app accesses

  • Location: When you run a recording session, the app accesses your device’s GPS location to save the coordinates of each point you log. Location is accessed only while a session is active.

How your data is stored

  • All sessions and points are stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not transmit your location or session data to the developer or any third party.
  • The app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking.

Sharing

  • You can export a session (GPX/CSV) with the in-app Share button. The data then goes only to the destination you choose. The developer never receives it.

Permissions used

  • Location (fine/coarse): to record GPS coordinates.
  • Foreground service, notifications, wake lock: to keep recording reliably while a session runs, including with the screen off.
  • Vibrate: to confirm a recorded point.

Data retention & deletion

  • Your data stays on your device until you delete a session in the app or uninstall the app.

Children

  • The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information.
