TapTrack — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

TapTrack (“the app”) is a GPS field-survey app that logs the locations of points you tap. This policy explains what the app accesses and how your data is handled. In short: your survey data stays on your device, and there is no account or sign-in.

What the app accesses

  • Location: while a session is open, the app reads your device’s GPS location to save the coordinates of each point you log.
  • Camera & photos: when you choose to attach a photo to a point, the app uses your camera. The photo (with its GPS location and time) is saved with the session on your device. The app does not access your existing photo gallery.

How your data is stored

  • All sessions, points, photos, and notes are stored locally on your device.
  • The app has no user accounts and no sign-in.
  • The app does not send your sessions, points, photos, or notes to the developer or to any server.
  • The app contains no advertising and no analytics or tracking.

Maps

  • To draw the map, the app downloads map tiles from OpenStreetMap (openstreetmap.org). As with any map app, these requests reveal the map area you are viewing and your device’s IP address to OpenStreetMap, handled under the OpenStreetMap Foundation’s privacy policy. No account or personal identifier is sent.

Purchases

  • An optional one-time “Pro” purchase (which removes a watermark from exported reports) is handled entirely by Google Play. TapTrack never sees or stores your payment details; the transaction is governed by Google’s privacy policy.

Sharing & export

  • You can export a session with the in-app Share button (an HTML report, plus GeoJSON, CSV, GPX, KML, and your photos). The data goes only to the destination you choose — the developer never receives it. The HTML report may include a baked-in map image built from OpenStreetMap tiles, as described under “Maps” above.

Permissions used

  • Location (fine/coarse): to record GPS coordinates.
  • Camera: to take photos you attach to points (only when you tap the camera).
  • Internet & network state: to download map tiles and, optionally, to process a Google Play purchase.
  • Foreground service, notifications, wake lock: to keep recording reliably while a session runs, including with the screen off.
  • Vibrate: to confirm a recorded point.

Data retention & deletion

  • Your data stays on your device until you delete a session in the app or uninstall the app.

Children

  • The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information.
